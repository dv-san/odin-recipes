Paella

You will need:

  • 500g paella rice
  • Tomato puree
  • Spicy chorizo
  • Grated cheese

Let's get started:

  1. Stick the paella rice in a pan or work and pour 1l boiling water over it
  2. Cook until al dente
  3. Squirt in the tomato puree and add some spices if ya like
  4. Chop the chorizo into chunky slices and throw it in the pan
  5. Serve with grated cheese
