Macaroni

You will need:

  • 500g Dried Macaroni pasta
  • Dolmio Tomato & Pepperoni stir-in sauce
  • 10 slices of pepperoni
  • 200g diced chicken breast
  • Grated cheese
  • Tobasco sauce

Let's get started:

  1. Chuck the pasta in a pot of water and bring to the boil
  2. Slice the chicken breast into strips and slap it in a pan
  3. Once the chicken starts to brown, stick the pepperoni in with it
  4. Pour the Dolmio stir-in sauce into the pan
  5. Drain the pasta and stick it in the pan with the rest of it
  6. Keep on the heat for 5mins
  7. Serve with some grated cheese and a splash of Tobasco
