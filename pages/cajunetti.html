Cajunetti

You will need:

  • 500g dried Linguine pasta
  • Cajun seasoning
  • Spicy chorizo
  • 200g diced chicken breast
  • Grated cheese

Let's get started:

  1. Stick the linguine in pan full of water and bring to the boil
  2. Drain the linguine, shove it back in the pan and throw some Cajun seasoning at it
  3. Brown the chicken off in a pan
  4. Chop the chorizo into chunky slices and throw it in the pan
  5. Serve with grated cheese
