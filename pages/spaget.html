Spaget

You will need:

  • 500g Dried Linguine pasta
  • Smooth Bolognese sauce
  • 12 meatballs
  • Garlic bread
  • Grated cheese
  • Tobasco sauce

Let's get started:

  1. Chuck meatballs and sauce in a slowcooker and leave it all day
  2. Stick the linguine in pan full of water and bring to the boil
  3. Place the garlic bread in the middle of the oven on a non-stick tray
  4. Drain the linguine abd top it with a couple ladel-fulls of meatballs and sauce
  5. Serve with some grated cheese and a splash of Tobasco
