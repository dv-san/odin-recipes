Spinacci

You will need:

  • 500g Dried Lingune Pasta
  • 125g Baby Leaf Spinach
  • Red Pesto
  • Sliced Spicy Chorizo
  • Cream Cheese

Lets get started:

  1. Bring the pasta to the boil and drain it before slinging it in a pan
  2. Splash in five table spoons of pesto
  3. Throw in as many chorizo slices as you fancy
  4. Chuck in a couple handfulls of spinach
  5. Stick two or three dollops of cream cheese in the pan
  6. Cook it up until the spinach shrivels and the chorizo starts to shrink
  7. Serve with a pinch of grated cheddar cheese
